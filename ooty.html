Ooty Trip Itinerary

Duration: 2 Days / 3 Nights

Train Ticket

Book a sleeper train ticket: Kacheguda Mysore Superfast Express from Kacheguda to Mysore for ₹435/-

Ride from Mysore to Ooty

At Mysore train station, book a ride to Ooty for 3 days. It costs ₹12,000 to ₹20,000 depending on the car you choose.

Attractions to see:

  • Mysore Palace (10 mins away from Mysore station)
  • Bandipur Wildlife Sanctuary

Then you reach Ooty.

Places to Visit in Ooty

  • Pykara Lake
  • Pykara Waterfalls
  • Rose Garden
  • Ooty Lake
  • Lamb's Rock
  • Toy Train Ride Experience

Accommodation in Ooty

  • Hostels start from ₹500/-
  • Homestays start from ₹2500/-

Food Places in Ooty

  • MJR Bakery
  • Darjeeling Spicy Momo
  • Le Cafe Ooty
  • Angara Restaurant
